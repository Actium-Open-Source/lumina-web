Privacy Policy

Lumina, made and owned by Actium uses your information as listed below. Feel free to reach out us on email.

3rd Party Interactions

How your data interacts with third party services

  • Your full name, preferred name, email and profile picture is obtained and stored by Lumina when signed in via Google.
  • Upon giving permission, Lumina will have access to a users Google Drive. This can be disabled at any time.
  • Lumina will be able to view, edit, and interpret a Google Document upon being provided.
  • OpenAI's 'ChatGPT 3.5' Large Language Model is provided written speeches or documents in order to provide feedback. This can be disabled at any time.

How We Use Your Data

How Lumina handles, processes, and uses your data

  • Your full name, preferred name, preferred pronouns, email, profile picture are saved and recorded upon being provided. These can be changed at any time.
  • Your messages on the Lumina application and web services are recorded. They are not actively monitored.
  • Your files and files you contribute to are linked to you and can be used as terms of service violations if deemed so.
  • Contribution history is recorded on files in order to responsibly uphold our terms of service.
